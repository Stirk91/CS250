Lab02: Questions and Answers
Linden Stirk

1. What are the four levels of exception safety?
No-throw guarantee, Strong exception safety, Basic exception safety, and No exception safety

2. How many catch blocks can there be with a single try block?
As many catch blocks as needed.

3. Which exceptions are children of the logic error exception?
invalid_argument, length_error, out_of_range

4. Which exceptions are children of the runtime error exception?
range_error, overflow_error, underflow_error
